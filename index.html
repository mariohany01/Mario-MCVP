  • Biography

    • Super Mario’s Journey

      Hey! It's Mario Hany, but call me Super Mario! I'm 23. Lived in Maadi, Egypt for like 6 years, then bounced to October. Started school at Cairo Language School, first couple grades, then moved again to October Gardens, Orascom Deutsch School! Did the whole Goethe thing, A1, A2, B1, yeah, trying to get those levels up, gotta travel, you know? Thanaweya amma scared the crap out of me. Switched to an American school. SAT 1 1400/1600! Nailed it for American schools.

    • From Mechatronics to Music

      Always wanted to be an architect, like, since forever. But when I hit MUST in 2019, went into engineering, mechatronics, and that was the best choice ever. First couple years? Brutal! American school to Egyptian engineering, HUGE jump. Got used to it though. Third year, started the Formula Student team, FSAE, building an F1 car, right? Meetings, work, 5 months, software, testing... then, no funding. Club deleted. Sad times. Piano? Started at 6, Dr. Maher, amazing teacher, orchestra leader from Netherlands. Taught me the basics, then he passed away. Miss that dude. Kept learning on my own, YouTube, self-taught, still at it. Got my own band, "صدفه" (Sodfa), five of us, piano, guitar, drums, bass, vocals. Parties in cafes, universities, you name it. First big one? NUSU opening. Then AUC Got Talent, finals, didn't win, oh well.

    • AIESEC Adventures

      In 2019, I fell in love and planned a future, but a 2022 breakup left me heartbroken. Hisham Saqr suggested AIESEC, and though unsure, I joined to distract myself. Starting as a top performer at AIESEC in MUST (-Holmies-), I broke records and became a Team Leader in OGV (-SuperMarios-), learning about leadership. I later took on roles like VP Finance for Fusion'23 (-OC سرينه Vol.1-) and OCP for Ignite'24 (-OC Bermuda-), gaining valuable experience and fixing past mistakes. I continued as VP Finance for Momentum'24 (-OC سرينه Vol.2-), learning finance and teamwork with great coaches like Adham Ashraf and Ziad Maher. After Momentum, I became OCP for Fusion'24 (-OC شفره-), a challenging but rewarding national-level experience. I then joined RPM (-OC حورس-) as IM, mastering Google Sheets, AppScript, and even creating a mobile app for inventory. I also helped Mondy’s team expand AIESEC to New Capital, a new startup, and applied for LCP. Now, I’m eager for my next AIESEC adventure, seeking more growth, connections, and opportunities in Egypt or abroad.
